ArtiStitch Body Art was founded in 2015 by Stitch McIntosh. After nearly a decade creating body-centered art across Portland, Stitch envisioned a space that captured his passion for experimental visuals and the weirdness that defines the city’s soul.

Our studio is small, immersive, and built around the experience of transformation. Our body art collective pushes artistic and cultural boundaries—using light, paint, and imagination to challenge perception and celebrate the canvas of the human form.
