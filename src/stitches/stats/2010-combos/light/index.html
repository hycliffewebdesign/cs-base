About us

Welcome to Our Stitch Skin Dermatology

Dr. Suchismita (Tia) Paul is a Harvard-MIT trained, board-certified dermatologist, a media skincare expert and an advisor/brand expert for several global skincare companies. She received her bachelor’s degree from Cornell University and a joint MD from Harvard Medical School and MIT, followed by her dermatology residency at the University of Miami, where she was a chief resident.

Aside from seeing patients, Dr. Paul is dedicated to patient advocacy and education on evidence-based medicine in dermatology, especially for skin of color. She has published several articles in top medical journals, such as Nature and Science, and has also been featured in media and various beauty and lifestyle publications including Insider, Huff Post, The Washington Post and Elle Magazine.

She has a social media following of more than 400K followers across platforms such as Instagram and TikTok where she educates and raises awareness on skincare and skin related issues.
