How It Works

From First Call to Live Site

A simple process designed for busy owners. Most brochure sites launch in a few weeks depending on content and feedback.

  1. 1

    Discovery Call

    We learn about your services, service area, and goals — then recommend the right pages and plan.

  2. 2

    Design & Build

    We craft your home page and wire up core pages with your branding, photos, and contact forms.

  3. 3

    Review & Launch

    You approve the site, we connect your domain, and run through a pre-launch performance checklist.

  4. 4

    Ongoing Support

    Need copy updates, new photos, or seasonal promos? Monthly plans include ongoing edits and hosting.
